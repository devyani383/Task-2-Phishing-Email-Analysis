# Task 2: Analyze a Phishing Email Sample

## Objective

Identify phishing characteristics in a suspicious email sample.

## Sample Email Analyzed

**From:** People's Bank [[service@peoples.com](mailto:service@peoples.com)]

**Subject:** Update Notification

### Message Summary

The email claims that there have been identity theft attempts and asks customers to confirm their banking details through a provided link. It warns that failure to do so may result in account suspension.

---

# Phishing Indicators Found

## 1. Creates Fear and Urgency

The email states that the account may be suspended if action is not taken immediately.

**Indicator:** Urgent language designed to pressure the user.

## 2. Requests Sensitive Information

The email asks users to confirm banking details.

**Indicator:** Legitimate banks do not request sensitive information through email.

## 3. Suspicious Link

The email instructs users to click a link to verify account details.

**Indicator:** Phishing emails often redirect users to fake websites that steal credentials.

## 4. Generic Greeting

The email uses "Dear People's Bank Customer" instead of the customer's actual name.

**Indicator:** Mass phishing campaigns commonly use generic greetings.

## 5. Social Engineering

The attacker exploits fear of identity theft and account suspension to manipulate the victim.

**Indicator:** Psychological manipulation to force immediate action.

## 6. Threat of Account Suspension

The email warns that the account may be temporarily suspended.

**Indicator:** Common phishing tactic used to create panic.

## 7. Sender Verification Required

Although the sender appears to be from People's Bank, the authenticity of the sender cannot be confirmed.

**Indicator:** Attackers often spoof trusted organizations.

---

# Conclusion

This email contains multiple phishing characteristics including:

* Urgent language
* Fear tactics
* Request for sensitive information
* Suspicious verification link
* Generic greeting
* Social engineering techniques
* Threat of account suspension

**Final Verdict:** This email is a phishing attempt designed to steal banking information from customers.
